# Medieval Vanilla Transport & Shuttle

Moves vanilla "Transport pod" and "Shuttle" research into the medieval era, chained under Medieval Overhaul's Engineering research: Engineering -> Transport pod -> Shuttle.

- Transport pod: tech level Medieval, requires Medieval Overhaul's Engineering (drops its Microelectronics/Biofuel/Machining prerequisites), researched at Medieval Overhaul's advanced research bench.
- Shuttle: tech level Medieval, requires Transport pod (drops its Multi-analyzer requirement), researched at the advanced research bench.

If Medieval Transport is also active, its reskinned pod/airship buildings are rerouted onto these vanilla research projects and its duplicate "medieval drop pods" / "medieval airship" research is removed - so you keep the medieval skins without the duplicate tech nodes.

Requires Medieval Overhaul (for the Engineering research and advanced research bench) and the Odyssey DLC for the Shuttle research.
